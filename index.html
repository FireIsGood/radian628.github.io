About Me

Here are my projects.

Raymarching Engine

A 3D renderer powered by distance fields. Supports realtime global illumination (that cool thing that raytracers do).

Voxel Life

A video exploring of Life-like Cellular Automata in 3D. I also composed the music.

Desmos Plane

A 3D game made in Desmos (and finalist in the 2021 art contest) where you fly a plane through a scenic landscape.

Photorealistic Desmos Fractals

A video showcasing photorealistic fractals I made, computed entirely in Desmos Graphing Calculator.

Nebula Fractals

The ends of three-dimensional ternary trees rendered with WebGL to create fractals resembling nebulae.

Control Freak

A tower defense game my friend Will and I made in 24 hours. The twist? Each tower only fires when you press its unique keybind.

Balls

Balls.

D&D 5e Encounter Tracker

A tool for tracking HP and status effects of creatures in D&D 5e, fit with tons of quality-of-life features and a builtin dice roller.

Photo Section Splitter

A tool for submitting handwritten assignments digitally that need to be split per-problem.

3D Fractals

An old version of my raymarching engine, made for viewing a specific type of fractal. Compatible with older hardware.

"Ducks" Fractal

Hardware-accelerated fractal generator for the "Ducks" fractal. Control w/ left/right mouse buttons and the scroll wheel.

LISPsmos

A highly-extensible programming language that compiles to Desmos expressions.

Divide and Conquer

An old RTS/Puzzle game I made in 2019 where you command a swarm of units against an enemy.

Edge-Replacement Fractal Generator

Make fractals (Dragon Curve, Levy C Curve, etc.) by recursively replacing edges with smaller sets of edges.
